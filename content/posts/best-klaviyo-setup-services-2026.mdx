## What Good Klaviyo Setup Looks Like

Not all setups are equal. Here's what a properly configured Klaviyo account includes — use this as a checklist whether you hire an agency or go the DIY route.

### Core Automated Flows

At minimum, you need these flows built, segmented, and tested:

- **Welcome Series** (email + SMS) — 4-7 messages, segmented by acquisition source
- **Abandoned Cart** — multi-step with dynamic product blocks and urgency triggers
- **Browse Abandonment** — triggered by viewed-but-not-purchased behavior
- **Post-Purchase** — cross-sell, review request, education content based on product purchased
- **Win-Back / Re-Engagement** — tiered by days since last purchase
- **Sunset Flow** — suppress unengaged contacts to protect deliverability

### Segmentation Architecture

Good segmentation goes beyond "engaged vs. unengaged." Your setup should include:

- **RFM-based segments** (recency, frequency, monetary) for campaign targeting
- **Engagement tiers** (30/60/90/120+ day windows) for send frequency management
- **Purchase behavior segments** — first-time buyers, repeat customers, VIPs, at-risk
- **Predictive segments** using Klaviyo's built-in predictive analytics (expected date of next order, predicted CLV)

### Deliverability Foundation

- Domain authentication (SPF, DKIM, DMARC) verified and passing
- Dedicated sending domain configured
- IP/domain warm-up plan executed (especially for migrations)
- Suppression lists imported and maintained
- Bounce and complaint handling automated

### Integrations

Your Klaviyo account should be connected to your full stack:

- Ecommerce platform (Shopify, BigCommerce, WooCommerce)
- Reviews platform (Yotpo, Judge.me, Okendo)
- Loyalty program (Smile.io, LoyaltyLion)
- Subscription tools (Recharge, Skio)
- Customer support (Gorgias, Zendesk)
- Analytics (GA4, Triple Whale, Northbeam)

## How to Choose the Right Klaviyo Setup Service

Picking the right agency comes down to four factors:

### 1. Match the Agency to Your Stage

Don't hire an enterprise agency if you're doing $300K in revenue. Don't hire a scrappy startup shop if you're processing 50,000 orders a month. The best klaviyo setup service for you is the one that regularly works with brands at your scale.

### 2. Verify Klaviyo Partner Status

Klaviyo's partner program has tiers — Partner, Gold, Master. Higher tiers mean the agency has demonstrated more platform expertise and client results. It's not the only factor, but it's a useful filter.

### 3. Ask About Their Process, Not Just Deliverables

"We'll build your flows" is table stakes. Ask about their audit process, how they approach segmentation strategy, how they handle deliverability, and what happens after setup. The best agencies have a documented methodology, not just a Trello board.

### 4. Check for Ecommerce Specificity

General marketing agencies that "also do Klaviyo" are a red flag. You want a team that specializes in ecommerce email/SMS and has case studies from brands in your vertical. Ecommerce has unique dynamics — seasonality, product launches, inventory-driven campaigns — that generalists often miss.

### 5. Understand What's Included Post-Setup

Some agencies hand you a configured account and wave goodbye. Others include 30-60 days of monitoring, optimization, and performance reporting. The post-setup period is when you find out if everything actually works under real traffic. Make sure support is included.
