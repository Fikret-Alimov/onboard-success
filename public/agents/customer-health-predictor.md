# Customer Health Predictor Agent

## Overview
This agent predicts customer health by analyzing behavioral signals, usage patterns, and engagement metrics. It helps Customer Success teams identify at-risk customers and prioritize interventions.

## Impact
- Reduce churn by early intervention
- Optimize resource allocation for CSMs
- Increase upsell and renewal rates

## Implementation Steps
1. Collect multi-modal customer interaction data.
2. Build predictive models using machine learning on historical data.
3. Continuously update health scores based on real-time activity.
4. Generate alerts and health dashboards for CSM teams.
5. Integrate with CRM and customer success platforms.
