# OnboardSuccess.com – GTM Strategy

**Author:** Go-to-Market Strategist  
**Site:** [OnboardSuccess.com](https://onboardsuccess.com)  
**Company:** Onboard Success OÜ  
**Audience:** CS Leaders, VPs of CS, CS Ops, CCOs (mid-market B2B SaaS)  
**Revenue Model:** Featured listings, affiliate links, sponsored content, lead gen

---

## 1. Launch Plan: First 30/60/90 Days

### **Days 1-30: Foundation & Seeding**
- **Website/UI:** Finalize a clean, conversion-oriented design for `/playbooks`, `/agents`, `/integrators`.
- **Content:** Publish 5 cornerstone playbooks (AI in SaaS CS use cases, implementation, vendor selection, etc).
- **Directories:**
  - Populate `/agents` with 30-40 credible AI CS tools.
  - Populate `/integrators` with 15-20 agencies (initially free listings).
- **Outreach (Initial):**
  - Soft outreach to tool vendors & agencies (email & LinkedIn), position as free early-stage listing.
  - Identify initial affiliate programs for tools.
- **SEO & Analytics:** Setup Google Search Console, Analytics, Bing Webmaster, and initial on-page SEO.

### **Days 31-60: Traction & Partnerships**
- **Content:** +8-10 new playbook articles.
- **Outreach:**
  - Begin active outreach for tool vendors to secure affiliate partnerships.
  - Begin pitching featured integrator listings to agencies (early adopters at a discount).
- **Distribution:** Start regular posts in communities, LinkedIn, and target newsletters.
- **Newsletter:** Launch weekly digest. First lead magnet (e.g., "AI Onboarding Tools Comparison 2024").
- **Backlinks:** Start guest posting (1-2 pieces), request inclusion in roundups.
- **Product:** Roll out "Request a Quote" integration (Zapier/Google Forms or similar).

### **Days 61-90: Monetization & Growth Loops**
- **Content:** +10 playbooks (case studies, trending AI workflows, interviews).
- **Experiment:** Sponsored playbook offers; measure initial interest.
- **Revenue:**
  - Continue converting integrators to featured listings, nudge freemium to paid.
  - Push affiliate-linked tools higher in agent directory.
- **Distribution:** Partner newsletter swaps & joint webinars.
- **SEO:** Build more robust link building (HARO, SaaS guest posts).
- **Scale**: Outreach to new agency/tool prospects informed by early traction.

## 3. Distribution Channels

### **LinkedIn**
- **Personal profile:** Solo founder posts wins, lessons, new content, directory milestones.
- **Company page:** Share playbooks, tool/agency spotlights, integration updates.
- **Targeted outreach:** Connect with CS leaders at SaaS companies, engage on their content.

### **Reddit**
- Engagement in /r/SaaS, /r/customersuccess, /r/b2b, /r/saas_talks.
- Focus: sharing non-promotional, actionable insights. Drop links only when highly relevant (e.g. “resource list” posts).

### **Communities**
- **SaaS/CS Slack Groups:** e.g. Gain Grow Retain, CS in Focus.
  - Share playbooks, invite feedback and contributors.
- **Facebook/Discord Groups:** Occasional resource shares on launch/new features.
- **Product Hunt launch:** When directory is >30 agents + 15+ integrators.

### **Newsletters**
- **Curated lists:** Apply for inclusion in “best of CS/AI” digests.
- **Direct partnerships:** Swap newsletter mentions with SaaS/CS adjacent newsletters.

## 4. Tool Vendor Outreach Plan (/agents)

- **Step 1:** Create Airtable of 100+ CS-focused AI vendors, including:
    - Tool Name, Website, Contact, Affiliate availability, LinkedIn, Integrator partners.
- **Step 2:** Outreach Email (personalized, template-based)
    - Value: Free listing, direct mid-market SaaS CS traffic, optional affiliate links.
    - Ask: Confirm description, add affiliate info/partner assets.
    - CTA: Reply or use intake form (Tally/Typeform).
- **Step 3:** Follow-up via LinkedIn message if no email reply in 5-7 days.
- **Step 4:** Integrate affiliate link/UTM for revenue tracking.
- **Step 5:** Feature top 3 tools monthly in newsletter and LinkedIn.

---

## 5. Agency Outreach Plan (/integrators)

- **Step 1:** Build database of >50 agencies offering AI/integration services for SaaS CS.
    - Agency Name, Contact, LinkedIn, Primary Markets, Pricing, Potential for Affiliate/Sponsorship.
- **Step 2:** Personalized email outreach
    - Value: Free listing + “Request a Quote” lead gen, highly targeted CS audience.
    - Offer: Early adopter featured listing at $39 (vs $79 later), 60-day money-back guarantee.
    - CTA: Intake form & call slot for premium onboarding.
- **Step 3:** Share playbook/social posts featuring their expertise (with backlink).
- **Step 4:** Monthly “Top Integrators” badge for highest engagement (drives FOMO).

## 7. Email List Growth Tactics

- **Lead Magnet:** Download ("AI Onboarding Tools Comparison 2024") — strong gated offer.
- **Exit-Intent Popup:** "Stay ahead on AI in CS — weekly tactics and tools, direct to you.”
- **Content Upgrades:** Cheat sheets & checklists for articles (“AI Playbook PDF”).
- **Newsletter Sign-up:** CTAs after each directory review, plus mid-article containers.
- **Social Previews:** Share exclusive newsletter-only insights/benchmarks to incentivize signup.
- **Referral Loop:** Give early access to datasets or job boards in exchange for email referrals.
- **Co-marketing:** Launch with partners/newsletters in the space (see below).

## 8. Partnership Opportunities

- **Tool Vendors:** Joint webinars, content swaps, “expert review” placements.
- **Agencies:** Co-branded playbooks (“How Top Agencies Implement AI in CS”), cross-promotion.
- **SaaS/CS Newsletters:** Swap mentions, sponsor a feature or curated list.
- **Industry Associations:** Seek resource-sharing or inclusion in toolkit pages.
- **Event Organizers:** Become a resource sponsor, e.g., “Official AI CS Toolkit by OnboardSuccess”.

## 9. Metrics to Track

- **Traffic:** Unique visitors, playbook/article views, directory page views (Google Analytics).

- **Listings:** Number of agents/agencies listed, new affiliate partnerships, featured listings sold.
- **Engagement:** Newsletter subscribers, email open rates, click-through rates, lead gen form submissions.
- **Revenue:** Affiliate earnings, featured listing income, sponsored content sales.
- **Outreach:** Cold email response rates, LinkedIn connection acceptances, partnership agreements.
